Frequently Asked Questions

What is Netflix?

Netflix is a popular subscription-based streaming service that allows you to watch a wide variety of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more on various internet-connected devices. Founded in 1997 by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph, Netflix has grown to become a global platform offering content in multiple languages.You can access Netflix on smart TVs, game consoles, streaming media players, smartphones, tablets, and computers. Depending on your subscription plan, you can also download content to watch offline. Netflix is known for its extensive library, including award-winning original series and films.

How much does Netflix cost?

Standard with Ads:

$6.99 per month. This plan includes ads and allows streaming on 2 devices simultaneously in 1080p (Full HD) resolution.

Standard:

$15.49 per month. This plan is ad-free and also allows streaming on 2 devices simultaneously in 1080p (Full HD) resolution. You can add one extra member for $7.99 per month.

Premium

$22.99 per month. This plan is ad-free, supports streaming on 4 devices simultaneously in 4K (Ultra HD) + HDR resolution, and allows up to 2 extra members for $7.99 each per month.

Where can I watch?

You can watch Netflix on a variety of devices, including:

Smart TVs:

Many smart TVs come with the Netflix app pre-installed.

Streaming Devices:

Devices like Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, Roku, and Chromecast support Netflix.

Mobile Devices:

You can watch on smartphones and tablets using the Netflix app.

Computers

Stream directly from the Netflix website on any computer.
